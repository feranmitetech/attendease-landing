Features

Everything your school needs to track attendance

No more paper, no more manual errors. AttendEase automates the entire process from check-in to parent notification.

QR code check-in

Every student gets a printed QR card. They scan it at the gate kiosk — attendance recorded in under a second.

👤

Facial recognition

Students walk past a camera and are identified automatically. No cards, no queues, no contact needed.

💬

Instant SMS alerts

Parents receive an SMS the moment their child is marked absent or arrives late. No more end-of-day surprises.

📊

Attendance reports

Track weekly trends, class performance, and chronic absentees. Export to CSV with one click.

🏫

Multi-role access

Admins, teachers, and principals each get their own view. Teachers only see their class. Principals see school-wide data.

Works on any device

How it works

Up and running in one day

No IT team needed. If you can use WhatsApp, you can set up AttendEase.

1

Register your school

Create your school account in 2 minutes. Add your classes and staff accounts.

2

Enroll students

Take each student's photo and generate their QR card. Print and laminate — done.

3

Mount the kiosk

Fix an Android tablet at the school gate. Students scan their card every morning.

4

Sit back

Attendance records itself. Parents get SMS alerts. You get clean reports.
